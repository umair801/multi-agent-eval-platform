DATAWEBIFY REFUND POLICY — Version 2.1

1. ELIGIBILITY
Customers are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of the original purchase date, provided the service has not been used beyond the trial limits. Refunds requested after 30 days are not eligible unless a duplicate charge or billing error is confirmed.

2. DUPLICATE CHARGES
If a customer has been charged more than once for the same billing period, a full refund of the duplicate amount will be issued within 5 to 7 business days. The customer must provide the invoice number and transaction date for both charges.

3. REFUND PROCESS
To initiate a refund, a refund ticket must be created by a support agent. The ticket is reviewed within 2 business days. Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Refunds cannot be issued to a different payment method than the one originally charged.

4. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS
Setup fees, onboarding fees, and custom integration fees are non-refundable. Partial months of service are not refundable after the 30-day window.

5. EXCEPTIONS
Refunds outside the standard policy may be approved at the discretion of a senior support manager. These exceptions require escalation to a human agent and are documented with justification.

6. CANCELLATION
Customers who cancel within the 30-day window receive a full refund. Customers who cancel after 30 days will not be charged for the next billing cycle but will not receive a refund for the current period.
