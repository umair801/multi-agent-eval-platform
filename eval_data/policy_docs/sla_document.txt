DATAWEBIFY SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT — Version 1.4

1. UPTIME COMMITMENT
Datawebify commits to 99.9% monthly uptime for all production services. Scheduled maintenance windows are excluded from uptime calculations. Customers will receive 48 hours advance notice for scheduled maintenance.

2. SUPPORT RESPONSE TIMES
Priority 1 (Service Down): Response within 1 hour, resolution target 4 hours.
Priority 2 (Major Feature Impaired): Response within 4 hours, resolution target 24 hours.
Priority 3 (Minor Issue): Response within 1 business day, resolution target 5 business days.
Priority 4 (General Inquiry): Response within 2 business days.

3. SLA CREDITS
If uptime falls below 99.9% in any calendar month, affected customers are eligible for SLA credits as follows:
- 99.0% to 99.9% uptime: 10% credit of monthly fee
- 95.0% to 99.0% uptime: 25% credit of monthly fee
- Below 95.0% uptime: 50% credit of monthly fee
Credits are applied to the next billing cycle and are not redeemable for cash.

4. ESCALATION PROCESS
Customer escalations can be initiated by contacting support with the word ESCALATE in the subject or message. Escalated tickets are reviewed by a senior support manager within 2 business hours. Customers will receive a direct callback or email from the manager assigned to their case.

5. EXCLUSIONS
SLA commitments do not cover outages caused by: customer-side network failures, third-party service outages outside Datawebify's control, force majeure events, or actions taken by the customer that violate the terms of service.

6. ENTERPRISE SLA
Enterprise customers on annual contracts are eligible for custom SLA terms with higher uptime commitments and dedicated support channels. Contact your account manager for details.
